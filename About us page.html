SARCOA – MAIN DINING ROOM

Sarcoa is a comfortable, modern and versatile space to host any type of function.  Our main dining room includes a large stage with full lighting and sound system, microphones and podium available, as well as a beautiful baby grand piano– not to mention floor to ceiling windows showcasing one of the best views Hamilton has to offer.

Room Capacity:

Plated Events – up to 60 Guests

Reception-Style Events – No more than 100 Guests

 

WATERFRONT PATIO & CABANAS

The Waterfront Patio Cabanas offer a more casual and laid back atmosphere.  Consider renting one of our VIP Cabanas for your next birthday, office party, bachelor or bachelorette, bridal or baby shower, or just for a unique place to hangout on a hot summer night!  We have 5 Cabanas in total, which means you can host an event for over 100 guests.
